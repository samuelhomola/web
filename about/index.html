Our history

The Apollon production group consisting of Apollon Studio Bratislava founded in 1994 and Apollon Pictures Vienna founded in 2005 is an international film, TV and digital media production network, specializing in production of TV commercials, corporate films, high-end foodshots and 3D/CGI.

What we offer

With more than 28 years experience in the media and advertising industry, Apollon provides its clients with the most comprehensive range of high-end creative production solutions spanning across all available media platforms.

What you will get

Full-service production that begins with a brief and ends with the finished end-product, and ongoing support. We offer creative solutions research, consultations, budget and timing analyses, project management, shoot supervision and an in-house fully integrated digital postproduction.

How we work

Reliability, professional work and creative support is the key to long term satisfaction of our clients. Combining the best of technology and artistry, our talented creative teams create original, memorable and astonishing work which meets our clients' targets.

We're always open for creative minds and collaborations.
